Who We Are

We are making Health Care Complete, Available and Better for everyone

SAVANT Personal Care Home is a highly reputable and trusted private service provider known for its quality specialist health care. We provide the underlying support for our families in a warm inviting atmosphere. We are located in a quiet suburban Atlanta neighbourhood.

SAVANT Personal Care Home was birthed as a tribute to my mother and father whose lives taught me true compassion. Our goal is to bridge the gap between a family’s intervention and their loved one’s independence. We invited our families to come and participate during family fun nights.
We bring convenience and compassionate care to the surrounding community and the public at large. We continue transforming lives through continuous evidence based medical practice; from competitively well thought through procedures. We have always set our sight on transforming the calibre of healthcare services across the state through thoughtful, innovative, and accessible medical practice under one roof.

`Our Mission is to provide all the comforts of home and all the confidence in knowing that each resident is treated as our own family.`

ANDREA SMITH, SAVANT HealthCare
