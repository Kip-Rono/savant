Who are we

We are making Health Care Affordable, Available and Better for everyone

The Focus Hospital is a highly reputable and trusted Level 4 private medical service provider known for its quality specialist health care. The hospital is Located along in Githurai 45, next to ACK Githurai Church. We bring affordability, convenience and compassionate care to the surrounding community and the public at large. We continue transforming lives through continuous evidence based medical practice; from competitively thought through packages. We have always set our sight on transforming the calibre of healthcare services across the country through thoughtful, innovative, and accessible medical practice under one roof. Underpinned by its strong values, The Focus Hospital has developed a strong reputation for providing a wide range of comprehensive specialized treatment across diverse medical fields.

Department of Radiology, Imaging

Our facility has an ultra-modern set of radiology service systems and we have up to date imaging department with a diagnostic X-ray, and ultrasound. The radiology unit is operated by highly skilled and experienced specialists who infers the clinical pictures of the patients.

Department of Physiotherapy

We have personalized provision of physiotherapy sessions on case-by-case basis. We recognize that the need for rehabilitation is individual. Each session of the therapy includes close nursing care; and additional medical equipment depending on the case such as crutches, walkers, and any other type of equipment to ease recovery.

  • Cardiac Rehabilitation

  • Fall Prevention

  • Massage & Relaxation

  • Plaster Technology

Department of Dentistry

“Smile is the first thing. And teeth the second. Smile without a healthy teeth is empty. Any one who got those two things, he’s got it all”. At The Focus We offer quality Dental Services including Root Canal Procedure, Braces etc

Department of Pathology, Labaratory Medicine

We pride in having an ultra-modern, equipped class E Laboratory to serve the need for medical investigations. Staffed with a team of dedicated and highly experienced personnel who uphold all the necessary standards for best practice. Any tests that cannot be done on site are referred to leading laboratories that maintain similar high-quality and standards.

Department of Trauma, Accident, Emergency Unit

This department is staffed by highly specialized emergency response team; nurses and doctors whose role is to assess the patient, perform the required emergency investigations and provide the patient with the necessary immediate life-saving treatment before they get admitted for more definitive care
